About Me

I'm a graduate student at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore pursuing an M.Des in Product Design, Development & Management. Before that I completed a B.Tech in Computer Science at VIT Vellore — which means I'm equally comfortable sketching user flows and shipping React components.

My approach to design starts with evidence. Whether it's surveying 25 classroom users to inform a chair redesign, or building a web experiment to statistically validate a perceptual metric, I believe good design decisions should be traceable to real human behaviour.

Outside academics I serve as Class Representative and Secretary of DeScIn (Design Science & Innovation) at IISc, and lead the Singapore vertical for EntIISc's Global Relations team. Previously, I coordinated guest experience for Riviera at VIT — Asia's largest techno-cultural fest.
